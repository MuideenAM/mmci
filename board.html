1. WAHEED AKIN OLAJIDE
B.Sc. Hons Ife, MBA (Ibadan)
FCA, FSTAN, FMAN
Mayegun Akomolede of Ibadan.

CHAIRMAN

Waheed Akin Olajide was born on 21st June, 1950. He finished his secondary education at Lagelu Grammar School Ibadan in 1969. He read Chemistry at the then University of Ife (now OAU) and graduated in 1975. He became a Fellow of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria in 1986 having trained earlier at the then Coopers and Lybrand - an international firm of Chartered Accountants. He studied for the MBA programme at the University of Ibadan from 1989 to 1991 graduating as the best student ever in the programme. He is a honorary fellow of Science Teachers Association of Nigeria and Mathematical Association of Nigeria. Akin Olajide is a publisher. He retired as the Managing Director/Chief Executive of University Press PLC. (The first book publishing house to be quoted on the stock exchange) in 2005.

He is also an author with different books in Business Studies, Commerce and Islamic Studies.

He has written so many articles published in local and international journals. He is the Chairman of Muslim Model College, Ibadan.

2. ADEGOKE ARIKE
B.Sc. Hons (Ibadan), PGDE (Ibadan)

BOARD MEMBER

Adegoke Arike was born on 2nd January, 1955. She attended Sacred Heart Conventionary School Idikan Ibadan (1962 - 1968), she attended Mount Olivet Grammar School Bodija (1969 - 1973) and Igbo-Elerin Girls Secondary School Ibadan (1973 -1974). She served the NYSC in Ondo State (1980 - 1981). She also had her PGDE at the same university.

She is married to Alhaji S. A. and she is presently an Educational consultant.

Work Experience:

  • Educational Officer
  • As a classroom teacher at Queen's School, Ibadan
  • A Principal also at the same school
  • A Director of Education in Oyo State Ministry of Education in the following offices:
    • Head of Planning, Research & Statistics Department
    • Head of Schools, Higher and Non-Formal Education Department
    • Head of Science, Maths and Technical Department

    Member of NUT
