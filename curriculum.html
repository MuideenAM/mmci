Curriculum

MMCI operates a comprehensive curriculum in line with the Federal and Oyo State Ministries of Education guidelines. Since inception, we have been offering the following subjects at Junior Secondary School level; English language, Yoruba language, Social studies, Basic Science, Basic Technology, French, Mathematics, Agricultural Science, Arabic, Islamic Religious Studies/CRK, Cultural and Creative Art, Physical and Health Education (PHE), Computer Studies and Civic Education.
At the Senior Secondary School level, the school offers: Englisg language, Mathematics, Civic Education, Yoruba language, Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Further Mathematics, Computer studies/ICT, Technical Drawing, Literature-in-English, Geography, Government, IRK/CRK, Economics, Arabic and Book Keeping.

At the Senior Secondary School level, there are four(4) fields of study from which each student would be expected to choose one, depending on his/her aptitude, interest and potential area of specialisation.
The subjects in these fields of studies are the following:

  1. SENIOR SECONDARY SCIENCE & MATHEMATICS
    1. 1. Biology
    2. 2. Chemistry
    3. 3. Physics
    4. 4. Further Maths
    5. 5. Agriculture
  2. SENIOR SECONDARY TECHNOLOGY
    1. 1. Technical Drawing
    2. 2. General Metal Work
    3. 3. Basic Electricity
    4. 4. Electronics
    5. 5. Auto-Mechanics
    6. 6. Building Construction
    7. 7. Wood Work
    8. 8. Home Management
    9. 9. Foods & Nutrition
    10. 10. Clothing & Textiles.
  3. SENIOR SECONDARY HUMANITIES
    1. 1. Nigerian Language
    2. 2. Literature-in-English
    3. 3. Geography
    4. 4. Government
    5. 5. CRS/IRS
    6. 6. French
    7. 7. Arabic
    8. 8. Economics.
  4. SENIOR SECONDARY BUSINESS STUDIES
    1. 1. Accounting
    2. 2. Store Management
    3. 3. Office Practice
    4. 4. Insurance
    5. 5. Commerce.
  5. CORE SUBJECTS
    1. 1. English Language
    2. 2. General Mathematics
    3. 3. Civic Education
    4. 4. Computer Studies
    5. 5. Trade/Entrepreneurship Subject
    6. 6. Biology.

Each student is expected to choose a total of eleven subjects for Senior Secondary School One, after which two would be dropped at Senior Secondary School Two.
