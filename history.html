History of the School

In realization of one of the objectives of Muslim Association of Nigeria, the Ibadan branch of the association under the leadership of Alhaji S.O. Oladapo decided to set machinery in motion for the establishment of both Primary and Secondary Schools within the premises of the association" Thus in 1993, the approval for the takeoff of the Nursery/Primary school was given and the foundation of a block of 4 - classrooms to serve the primary school was laid under the supervision of Late Eng. Al- Ameen Atere.

At the commencement of the tenure of Alhaji Waheed Akin Olajide as the Chairman of the Association in 1998, being an educationist and a lover of Education, he followed his predecessor's pattern of seeing to the improvement of the Primary School and went further in the establishment of a private secondary school. The trio of Alhaji Akin Olajide, Alhaja Mutiat Oladapo and Alhaja Arike Adegoke worked tirelessly to see to the actualization of the dream and came up with the name, logo, motto, mission and vision statements for the school which were accepted and approved by the general house. Hence, the name of School: MSULIM MODEL COLLEGE, IBADAN and the Motto is "For Justice and Truth".

The school was approved by the Oyo State Minisiry of Education via its letter reference No: DR2/2254/22 of 13th August, 2001 and the school officially took off on September 17,2001 which was the founder's day.
